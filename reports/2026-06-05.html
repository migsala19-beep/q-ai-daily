Research suggests users inadvertently develop attachment through routine interactions rather than deliberately seeking companion chatbots. This calls for a fundamental reassessment of engagement metrics and ethical boundaries in product design. Meanwhile, multi-agent collaboration remains a key technical frontier, exemplified by SMAC-Talk, which extends the StarCraft benchmark with natural language commands to study team coordination and decentralized decision-making among LLMs, offering new evaluation standards for swarm intelligence in business operations.
