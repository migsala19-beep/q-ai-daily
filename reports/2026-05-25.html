Today's GitHub Trending reveals two AI-related undercurrents. First, the "self-hosted infrastructure" wave—memos, immich, and pocketbase signal strong demand for data sovereignty. Second, real-time data visualization is becoming essential. Perspective, designed for large streaming datasets, bridges the critical gap between model outputs and human decisions. As LLM agents generate massive structured data, low-latency real-time dashboards will become standard AI infrastructure.
