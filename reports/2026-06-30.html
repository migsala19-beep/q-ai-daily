🔬 深度研究报告

结合今日资讯，两个方向值得重点关注。

第一，Agentic 世界模型规划正从“反应式”走向“预测式”。arXiv 论文《Internalizing the Future: A Unified Agentic Training Paradigm for World Model Planning》提出统一训练范式，让 LLM 智能体在行动前进行“what-if”推理，评估潜在计划而非简单响应环境。这标志着智能体开始具备类人前瞻性，有望在复杂决策、机器人控制、自动驾驶等长程任务中突破现有瓶颈。

第二，AI 模型网络与多智能体协作成为新焦点。《AI-Model Network: Concept, Current State and Future》探讨模型互联协作的框架，而《When Does Personality Composition Matter for Multi-Agent LLM Teams?》则研究不同“个性”提示对团队任务结果的影响。两者结合预示未来 AI 系统将由多个异构模型组成，按角色分工完成复杂任务，而非依赖单一超级模型。
English
Two directions stand out today. First, agentic world-model planning is shifting from reactive to predictive. The paper "Internalizing the Future" proposes a unified training paradigm that enables LLM agents to conduct "what-if" reasoning and evaluate plans before acting. This human-like foresight could unlock long-horizon tasks such as robotics and autonomous systems.

Second, AI-model networks and multi-agent collaboration are gaining traction. "AI-Model Network: Concept, Current State and Future" explores interconnected model ecosystems, while "When Does Personality Composition Matter for Multi-Agent LLM Teams?" examines how personality prompting affects team outcomes. Together, they suggest future AI systems will orchestrate heterogeneous specialized agents rather than rely on one monolithic model.
