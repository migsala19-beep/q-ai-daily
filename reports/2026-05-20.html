🔬 深度研究报告

**趋势一：多智能体编排从"能跑"进入"可控"阶段。** 今日 GitHub Trending 中的 n8n 作为低代码工作流自动化平台，已集成 400+ 连接器和原生 AI 能力，代表工程侧的成熟落地。学术侧两篇论文直击痛点：SDOF 针对 LangChain/CrewAI 等框架缺乏业务流程状态约束的问题，提出状态约束调度以削减"对齐税"；SkillSmith 则将 Agent 技能编译为带边界检查的运行时接口，解决技能注入后的越权与冲突。两者共同指向一个信号——多 Agent 系统的下一个战场是可控性与工程规范，而非单纯的能力堆砌。

**趋势二：AI 生成内容的评价维度从"产物"扩展到"交付"。** DeepSlide 论文指出，现有 AI 幻灯片生成器过度优化视觉产物（artifact），却忽视了演讲节奏、叙事结构和准备过程。这揭示了一个更广泛的范式转移：随着大模型生成质量趋于饱和，"最后一公里"的交付体验（delivery）将成为差异化关键，适用于文档、代码、视频等所有生成场景。
English
**Trend 1: Multi-agent orchestration moves from "functional" to "controllable."** On the engineering side, n8n (trending on GitHub) offers a mature low-code workflow platform with 400+ integrations and native AI. On the research side, SDOF tackles the "alignment tax" in LangChain/CrewAI by enforcing state-constrained dispatch, while SkillSmith compiles agent skills into boundary-guided runtime interfaces to prevent skill conflicts. Together, they signal that the next battleground for multi-agent systems is control and engineering discipline, not raw capability stacking.

**Trend 2: AI-generated content evaluation shifts from artifact to delivery.** The DeepSlide paper argues that current AI slide generators optimize visual artifacts while neglecting pacing, narrative, and presentation preparation. This reflects a broader paradigm shift: as generative quality plateaus, the "last mile" delivery experience will become the key differentiator across documents, code, video, and beyond.

💡 实践启发

- 在内部自动化项目中引入 n8n 替代零散脚本，利用其可视化编排降低维护成本。
理由：400+ 集成与 AI 节点可快速搭建业务流程，减少重复开发。
- 若在使用 LangChain/CrewAI 做多 Agent 系统，可引入 SDOF 的状态约束思想。
理由：防止 Agent 链在无状态管控下产生越权调用或循环依赖。
- 设计 AI Agent 的技能库时，参考 SkillSmith 的边界接口模式。
理由：明确技能输入输出边界，降低运行时冲突与提示注入风险。
- 评估内部 LLM 应用效果时，加入交互式场景测试而非仅看基准分数。
理由：Theory of Mind 论文显示，故事阅读理解高分未必转化为更好的人机协作。
- 如果你在做 AI 演示/内容工具，把优化重心从"生成质量"扩展到"使用体验"。
理由：DeepSlide 表明，交付环节（节奏、叙事）才是用户真正感知价值的时刻。
English
- Adopt n8n for internal automation instead of scattered scripts.
Rationale: 400+ integrations and AI nodes reduce maintenance and speed up workflow building.
- Apply state-constrained dispatch (SDOF) in LangChain/CrewAI multi-agent setups.
Rationale: Prevents unauthorized calls and circular dependencies in agent pipelines.
- Design agent skill libraries with boundary-guided interfaces (SkillSmith).
Rationale: Clear input/output boundaries reduce runtime conflicts and prompt-injection risks.
- Evaluate LLM applications with interactive scenarios, not just benchmark scores.
Rationale: Theory of Mind research shows high story-task scores may not improve real collaboration.
- Shift optimization focus from generation quality to delivery experience in AI content tools.
Rationale: DeepSlide shows pacing and narrative are where users actually perceive value.
