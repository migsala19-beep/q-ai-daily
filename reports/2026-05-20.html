**趋势二：LLM"心理理论"与内部偏见机制成为研究热点**
两篇arXiv论文从相反方向切入LLM可信度。DeepSlide关注AI生成内容的"交付"维度而非仅"产出"，暗示评估框架正在扩展。关于Theory of Mind的研究则质疑现有基准（多为故事阅读/选择题）能否衡量真实交互场景中的心智推理。另一篇"Fair outputs, Biased Internals"揭示LLM输出层面公平但内部潜在表征存在因果不对称偏见。这些研究共同指向：下一代LLM评估必须穿透表层输出，深入内部状态与真实交互场景。
English
**Trend 1: AI Workflows Evolving from Chained Calls to Production-Grade State Management**
Today's GitHub leader n8n (fair-code platform with 400+ integrations) resonates with the arXiv paper SDOF. n8n represents the hottest low-code AI automation practice, while SDOF pinpoints a critical flaw in LangChain/LangGraph: they route tasks through graphs but fail to enforce stage constraints governing real business processes. SDOF introduces State-Constrained Dispatch, modeling multi-agent execution as state-machine-governed processes to reduce alignment tax. Concurrently, SkillSmith proposes compiling agent skills into boundary-guided runtime interfaces to prevent overreach. The convergence signals that the 2026 AI Agent competitive focus has shifted from "can it do it" to "can it run stably and controllably in production."

**Trend 2: LLM Theory of Mind and Internal Bias Causality Becoming Hotspots**
Two arXiv papers tackle LLM trustworthiness from opposite angles. DeepSlide emphasizes the "delivery" dimension of AI-generated content rather than mere artifact production, implying our evaluation frameworks are expanding. The Theory of Mind study questions whether existing benchmarks (mostly story-reading/multiple-choice) adequately measure mental reasoning in real interactive scenarios. The "Fair outputs, Biased Internals" paper reveals that LLMs may appear fair at the output level while harboring causally asymmetric biases in latent representations. Together, these studies conclude that next-generation LLM evaluation must penetrate beyond surface outputs into internal states and real interaction contexts.

- **在多Agent系统中引入"状态机约束"**：参考SDOF思路，在LangChain/LangGraph流程中显式定义阶段边界与状态转换规则，避免Agent越权导致一致性问题。
- **重新评估AI生成内容的质量维度**：不仅检查PPT视觉效果，更要检验叙事逻辑和交付适配性，借鉴DeepSlide的"交付导向"评估框架。
- **谨慎对待LLM的"心理理论"光环**：在客服、教育等强交互场景中，不要仅凭基准测试分数部署ToM功能，应先做真实用户A/B测试验证实际收益。
- **建立LLM输出的内部偏见审计机制**：表面公平不等于内部无偏，对高敏感场景（招聘、信贷）的LLM应用，应增加潜在表征层面的因果偏见检测。
English
- **Adopt n8n for internal AI automation pipelines**: With 400+ native integrations and self-hosting capability, it's ideal for data flow and lightweight agent workflows, reducing reliance on commercial SaaS.
- **Introduce state-machine constraints in multi-agent systems**: Following SDOF, explicitly define stage boundaries and state transition rules in LangChain/LangGraph flows to prevent consistency issues from agent overreach.
