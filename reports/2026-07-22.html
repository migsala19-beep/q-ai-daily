English
Today's strongest theme is "LLM alignment and safety evaluation." The arXiv paper "Rater State Bias in RLHF Preference Data: An Audit Framework" shows that preference labels are not only shaped by response quality but also by annotators' sustained stress or emotional states, creating a structural confound in reward modeling. Separately, "Some Large Language Models Exhibit Consistent Risk Attitudes" finds that certain LLMs display systematic risk preferences, which has implications for high-stakes domains like finance, healthcare, and autonomous systems. On GitHub, NVIDIA/Megatron-LM continues to advance large-scale Transformer training, while PINTO_model_zoo supports cross-framework model conversion for edge deployment. Overall, frontier research is increasingly focusing on human-in-the-loop contamination and the psychological properties of model decisions, while engineering efforts remain centered on training and deployment efficiency.

理由：心理状态会系统性偏置奖励模型，早期审计成本远低于事后修复。

理由：模型若对风险有稳定偏好，会放大业务决策偏差。

理由：PlanFlip 证明攻击可在规划阶段污染任务目标，隔离能降低影响面。
4. 关注 NVIDIA Megatron-LM 的分布式训练最佳实践。
理由：大规模训练仍是核心瓶颈，复用官方代码可节省工程试错。
5. 用 PINTO_model_zoo 做模型格式转换与边缘部署原型。
理由：跨框架转换库能降低从 PyTorch 到 ONNX/EdgeTPU 的迁移成本。
English
1. Add annotator-state questionnaires in RLHF projects to reduce preference-label noise.
Rationale: Rater states systematically bias reward models, and early auditing is cheaper than retrospective fixes.
2. Run risk-preference tests for LLMs used in high-stakes decisions.
Rationale: Stable risk attitudes in models can amplify business decision biases.
3. Design input isolation for the planning phase of multi-agent systems.
Rationale: PlanFlip shows that planning-stage injection can corrupt task objectives, and isolation limits blast radius.
4. Follow NVIDIA Megatron-LM's distributed training best practices.
Rationale: Large-scale training remains a core bottleneck; reusing official code saves engineering trial and error.
5. Use PINTO_model_zoo for model format conversion and edge-deployment prototypes.
Rationale: Cross-framework conversion lowers migration costs from PyTorch to ONNX/EdgeTPU.
