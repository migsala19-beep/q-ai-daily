On-device AI deployment is the strongest signal in today's GitHub trending. whisper.cpp ports OpenAI Whisper to C/C++, enabling local speech recognition on CPU, embedded devices, and even mobile phones—clear evidence of the "cloud-minimal" deployment trend. Meanwhile, openai/whisper remains on the list, confirming continued interest in robust speech foundation models. This aligns with the rise of self-hosted photo manager Immich and the Postgres platform Supabase, which together provide privacy-first, cost-transparent data and media backends for AI applications. Together, these trends sketch a practical AI stack: local inference, self-hosted data, and multi-language infrastructure.
