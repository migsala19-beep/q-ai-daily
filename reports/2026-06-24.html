两大趋势值得深入关注。其一，AI Agent正从实验工具转向正式工程角色。论文《Specifying AI-SDLC Processes》首次提出协议语言，规范人类与AI在软件开发生命周期中的责任边界、审批关卡与治理约束；PEAR论文则通过置换等变自适应路由优化多智能体辩论。两者共同指向：多智能体协作的工业化需要明确的协议与边界。其二，推理策略评估走向精细化。《Beyond Fixed Budgets》系统分析Tree-of-Thought在不同预算下的弹性与局限，揭示复杂搜索策略并非万能，资源约束下的策略选择比盲目堆叠更重要，呼应了业界从"更大模型"向"更优推理"的转向。
English
Two key trends emerge. First, AI agents are transitioning from experimental tools to formal engineering roles. The AI-SDLC paper proposes a protocol language defining human-agent responsibility boundaries and governance constraints in software development, while PEAR advances multi-agent debate through permutation-equivariant adaptive routing. Together they signal that industrial multi-agent collaboration requires clear protocols and boundaries. Second, reasoning strategy evaluation is maturing. The ToT paper systematically analyzes elasticity and limitations of tree search under fixed budgets, revealing that complex reasoning strategies are not universally superior—optimal strategy selection under resource constraints matters more than blindly scaling complexity, aligning with the industry shift from "bigger models" to "better inference."
