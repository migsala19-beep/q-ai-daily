🔬 深度研究报告

今日资讯突出两个方向：一是从反应式到主动式企业Agent，二是AI在垂直领域的可信落地。

主动式企业Agent代表新范式。论文《Context Graphs for Proactive Enterprise Agents》指出，当前RAG和Agent框架虽进步显著，但本质上仍是"用户提问后才行动"的反应式系统。真正的企业生产力提升需要Agent能基于组织上下文图主动识别任务、预测需求并提前执行。这要求Agent不仅能检索知识，还要构建跨部门、跨流程的上下文关联，类似于企业内部的"数字预警系统"。

医疗AI的可信化同样关键。《Aligning Clinical Needs and AI Capabilities》综述了LLM在医学推理中的进展与临床需求之间的差距；《Alignment Plausibility》则提出新的评估标准，强调AI输出不仅要准确，还要在临床决策路径中具备可解释性和可审计性。这标志着医疗AI正从"能力展示"转向"责任落地"。
English
Two directions stand out: the shift from reactive to proactive enterprise agents, and trustworthy AI deployment in vertical domains.

Proactive enterprise agents represent a new paradigm. The paper "Context Graphs for Proactive Enterprise Agents" argues that while RAG and agentic frameworks have advanced, they remain fundamentally reactive, waiting for human queries. True productivity gains require agents that can leverage organizational context graphs to identify tasks, anticipate needs, and act preemptively. This demands not just knowledge retrieval, but cross-functional context modeling—essentially a digital early-warning system inside the enterprise.

Trustworthy healthcare AI is equally important. "Aligning Clinical Needs and AI Capabilities" surveys the gap between LLM capabilities and clinical reasoning requirements, while "Alignment Plausibility" proposes a new assurance standard emphasizing explainability and auditability in clinical decision paths. This signals a shift from capability demonstration to accountable deployment.
