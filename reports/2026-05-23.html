第一，智能体正从"一次性部署"走向"终身自适应"。SOLAR论文直击动态环境中概念漂移与梯度微调成本高的痛点，提出自优化开放式智能体，让模型在无人工干预下持续适应环境变化。COSMO-Agent则将这一思路推向工业界，通过工具增强实现CAD-CAE闭环优化，自动将仿真反馈转化为几何编辑，显著压缩工业设计迭代周期。这标志着Agent技术开始穿透垂直行业核心 workflow。

English
Two key trends emerge today: lifelong learning for autonomous agents, and the industrialization of multi-agent orchestration.

First, agents are shifting from static deployment to lifelong self-adaptation. The SOLAR paper tackles concept drift and costly gradient-based updates in dynamic environments, proposing a self-optimizing open-ended agent that adapts continuously without human intervention. COSMO-Agent extends this to industry, using tool augmentation to close the CAD-CAE loop—automatically translating simulation feedback into geometric edits and compressing industrial design cycles. This signals Agent technology penetrating vertical core workflows.

Second, multi-agent collaboration is becoming the key to complex open-ended tasks. AgentCo-op introduces retrieval-based workflow synthesis, addressing the triple deficit of training data, evaluation metrics, and standardized interfaces in open science. On GitHub, Activepieces integrating ~400 MCP servers and LangChain's sustained popularity show academia and industry converging on "tool-augmented agents" and "multi-agent orchestration," indicating an ecosystem inflection point.
