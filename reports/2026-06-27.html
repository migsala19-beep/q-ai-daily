🔬 深度研究报告

今日资讯凸显两大技术方向。其一，模型行为的精细化控制进入新阶段。arXiv两篇论文分别从"谄媚"（Sycophancy）和"拒绝"（Refusal）切入，揭示通过激活空间操控（activation steering）可定向调节模型行为。尤其值得注意的是，"Refusal Lives Downstream of Persona"发现顺从型人设（compliant persona）会抑制模型的拒绝本能，这意味着安全与实用性并非零和，而可通过人设工程协同优化。其二，LLM作为"语义变异算子"正向高风险决策领域渗透。AlgoEvolve将LLM驱动的元进化从传统代码合成拓展至算法交易程序，利用LLM的语义理解能力生成并筛选交易策略，显示出LLM在需要连续优化和领域知识融合的场景中的通用性。这两项进展共同指向：LLM研究正从"能力展示"转向"可控性"与"专用化"的深层工程。
English
Two key directions emerge today. First, fine-grained control of model behavior is advancing. Two arXiv papers tackle sycophancy and refusal through activation steering, showing that model traits can be directionally tuned. Notably, "Refusal Lives Downstream of Persona" reveals that a compliant persona gates refusal, suggesting safety and utility can be co-optimized via persona engineering rather than treated as a trade-off. Second, LLMs as "semantic mutation operators" are penetrating high-stakes decision domains. AlgoEvolve extends LLM-driven meta-evolution from static code synthesis to algorithmic trading, leveraging LLMs' semantic understanding to generate and screen strategies. This demonstrates LLMs' versatility in scenarios requiring continuous optimization fused with domain knowledge. Together, these signal a shift from capability demonstration to deep engineering around controllability and specialization.

→ 理由：其文档代理能力已成熟，可直接对接企业知识库场景。

→ 理由：从源头降低隐私合规风险，符合日益严格的数据保护趋势。
4. 用LLM作为"策略进化引擎"，对现有业务规则（如定价、推荐策略）进行变异-评估-筛选迭代。
→ 理由：AlgoEvolve证明LLM可跨域应用，业务规则优化比代码生成更直接产生价值。
5. 重新设计内部AI评估体系，在准确率之外引入鲁棒性、公平性、可解释性等维度。
→ 理由：CORE-Bench研究警示单一指标饱和会掩盖真实能力缺陷。

1. Try activation steering to debug local open-source models and cheaply tune the personality-safety balance.
