English
Agentic AI is clearly transitioning from proof-of-concept to systematized engineering and reliability diagnostics. The paper *The Hitchhiker's Guide to Agentic AI* offers a full-stack practitioner's guide from first principles to production, signaling the community's urgent need for standardized frameworks. Meanwhile, *Diagnosing and Mitigating Compounding Failures in Agentic Persuasion* highlights how multi-agent systems suffer from error accumulation in long-horizon open-ended tasks, proposing taxonomic strategy retrieval as a mitigation path—shifting the focus from capability to reliability. On the tooling side, GitHub Trending shows dual-track growth: LlamaIndex (document agents and OCR) and MONAI (healthcare imaging) reflect AI's vertical penetration into knowledge management and specialized domains, while non-AI tools like CasaOS, NocoDB, and PowerToys dominate trending, revealing developers' strong dual demand for data sovereignty and productivity enhancement.
