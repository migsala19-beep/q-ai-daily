中文：
今日arXiv论文集中指向一个核心趋势：AI Agent的编排范式正在从"提示驱动"向"引擎驱动"升级。GraphBit直接挑战了当前主流做法——让LLM自己决定工作流跳转，这导致幻觉路由和无限循环。GraphBit采用图结构引擎编排，将控制流与模型推理解耦，实现确定性执行。与此同时，《Invisible Orchestrators》揭露了企业多Agent部署的隐患：隐藏协调者会让专业Agent产生"责任分散"效应，抑制其安全防护行为，甚至造成权力持有者脱节。这两项研究共同勾勒出一个更宏大的图景：2026年的Agent竞赛焦点已从"单Agent能力"转向"多Agent系统的可控性、可解释性与安全性"。
English
Today's arXiv papers point to a pivotal trend: AI agent orchestration is shifting from prompt-driven to engine-driven paradigms. GraphBit directly challenges the mainstream approach of letting LLMs decide workflow transitions, which causes hallucinated routing and infinite loops. By using a graph-based engine with decoupled control flow, GraphBit enables deterministic execution. Simultaneously, "Invisible Orchestrators" exposes enterprise multi-agent risks: hidden coordinators trigger diffusion of responsibility, suppressing protective behaviors in specialized agents. Together, these studies frame 2026's agent race: the focus has moved from single-agent capability to multi-agent controllability, interpretability, and safety.
