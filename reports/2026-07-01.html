🔬 深度研究报告

1. 多智能体系统与人格化提示：arXiv 论文《When Does Personality Composition Matter for Multi-Agent LLM Teams?》指出，人格提示会影响智能体沟通方式，但对客观任务结果的影响仍待探索。该方向提示未来构建企业级多智能体工作流时，不仅要设计角色分工，还要考虑“人格组合”对协作效率与冲突的系统性影响。适用于客服、研发、内容审核等需要多 Agent 协作的场景。

2. 世界模型与自主智能体训练：《Internalizing the Future: A Unified Agentic Training Paradigm for World Model Planning》提出将“未来推演”内化为训练范式，使 LLM Agent 从反应式决策转向“what-if”前瞻性规划。这与自动驾驶、机器人、复杂任务调度等长周期决策场景高度相关。配合 NVIDIA Megatron-LM 等大规模训练基础设施，未来可训练出具备世界模型能力的专用 Agent。
English
1. Multi-agent systems and personality prompting: The paper “When Does Personality Composition Matter for Multi-Agent LLM Teams?” shows that personality prompts alter agent communication, yet their impact on objective outcomes remains underexplored. This suggests enterprise multi-agent workflows should account for personality composition alongside role design, especially in customer support, R&D, and content moderation.

2. World models and agentic training: “Internalizing the Future: A Unified Agentic Training Paradigm for World Model Planning” proposes internalizing future simulation into agent training, shifting LLM agents from reactive to “what-if” planning. This is highly relevant for autonomous driving, robotics, and long-horizon scheduling. Combined with large-scale training frameworks like NVIDIA Megatron-LM, specialized agents with world-model capabilities could emerge.

- 关注世界模型+Agent 训练方向，提前储备长周期决策场景数据。理由：该方向是通用智能体从“反应”走向“规划”的关键。
- 将“本地可验证基础模型”纳入可信 AI 路线图。理由：Odyssey 框架为金融、医疗等高风险领域提供可解释、可验证的模型构建思路。
- 利用 free-for-dev 清单降低 infra 成本。理由：ripienaar/free-for-dev 是开发者选型免费云资源的经典参考。
- 关注隐私通讯赛道。理由：SimpleX Chat 等无用户标识的通讯协议符合全球数据隐私监管趋势。
