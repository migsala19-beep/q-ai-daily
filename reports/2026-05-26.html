二是 **神经符号主义与结构化知识的回归**。NeuroNL2LTL 通过神经符号框架将自然语言翻译为线性时序逻辑（LTL），直接服务于安全关键系统的形式化验证；SciAtlas 构建大规模科学知识图谱，以结构化方式对抗学术信息爆炸。这表明纯数据驱动的 LLM 正在与符号推理、知识工程深度融合，以提升可靠性并拓展在硬科学领域的应用边界。
English
Two key trends emerge today. First, **Agentic AI is penetrating professional research-level tasks**. RMA targets research-grade mathematical problems, moving beyond competition math or formal theorem proving. BOHM enables zero-cost hierarchical attribution for compound AI systems, addressing interpretability in multi-component pipelines. Energy per Successful Goal introduces goal-level energy accounting, tackling efficiency concerns for long-running agents. Together, they sketch next-generation professional agents: capable of complex reasoning, interpretable, measurable, and energy-efficient.

Second, **neuro-symbolic approaches and structured knowledge are making a comeback**. NeuroNL2LTL translates natural language into Linear Temporal Logic via a neuro-symbolic framework, directly supporting formal verification for safety-critical systems. SciAtlas builds a large-scale scientific knowledge graph to combat information explosion through structured organization. This signals that pure data-driven LLMs are converging with symbolic reasoning and knowledge engineering to improve reliability and expand into hard-science domains.
