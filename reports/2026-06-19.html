🔬 深度研究报告

今日资讯的核心主线是**Agentic AI 从通用对话向深度专业工作流的渗透**，以及**测试时计算（Test-time Compute）的范式扩展**。

在Agentic搜索方面，arXiv论文《Beyond Parallel Sampling》指出，当前Agent搜索的广度扩展依赖简单的并行采样，已出现边际收益递减。作者提出「多样性查询初始化」机制，通过差异化查询起点而非单纯增加rollout数量来提升搜索效率。这标志着Agent推理正从「堆深度（更多轮次）」或「堆宽度（更多线程）」的粗放模式，转向更精细的搜索空间结构设计。

在垂直领域落地方面，微软的Qlib平台集成了监督学习、市场动力学建模与强化学习，并引入RD-Agent自动化研发流程，显示AI量化投资进入「端到端自动化」阶段。与此同时，两篇供应链相关论文（SkillChain-Gym与Skill-Constrained MPC）将员工技能衰减、再培训耗时纳入生产-库存控制，填补了传统运筹学忽略人力资本动态演化的空白。法律领域的自进化Agent则进一步证明，结合BM25与动态规则学习的混合架构，在专业检索中优于单一 dense retrieval。
English
The dominant theme is **Agentic AI penetrating deep professional workflows**, alongside **structural innovations in test-time compute scaling**.

The arXiv paper *Beyond Parallel Sampling* reveals that naive breadth scaling via parallel rollouts yields diminishing returns for agentic search. The authors propose *diverse query initialization*—engineering differentiated starting points rather than merely adding threads—to improve search efficiency. This signals a shift from brute-force depth/width scaling toward more sophisticated search-space architecture.

On the vertical-application front, Microsoft’s Qlib now unifies supervised learning, market dynamics, and RL, while automating R&D with RD-Agent—pushing AI-driven quant research toward end-to-end automation. Meanwhile, two supply-chain papers (SkillChain-Gym and Skill-Constrained MPC) explicitly model workforce skill decay and retraining time within production-inventory control, filling a long-standing gap in operations research. The self-evolving legal agent further demonstrates that hybrid architectures combining BM25 with dynamic rule learning outperform pure dense retrieval in professional domains.
