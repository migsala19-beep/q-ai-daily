🔬 深度研究报告

今日资讯揭示两大明确趋势。其一是「自托管基础设施的全面复兴」：GitHub Trending 中 Coolify（开源PaaS）、Immich（照片视频管理）、Jellyfin（媒体系统）、n8n（工作流自动化）、Supabase（Postgres平台）集体上榜，说明开发者和企业正从"依赖公有云SaaS"转向"自有服务器可控部署"。驱动力有三——数据隐私合规、长期成本优化、以及对供应商锁定的规避。Coolify 直接对标 Vercel/Heroku，支持280+一键部署服务，标志着自托管体验已接近托管平台的便捷度。

其二是「AI Agent 从功能实现到治理安全」的范式跃迁：SDOF 论文提出在多Agent编排中引入状态约束调度，解决传统 LangChain/LangGraph 不强制业务流程阶段的"对齐税"问题；SkillSmith 则将 Agent 技能编译为带边界的运行时接口，防止技能被滥调或越权执行。两者共同指向一个信号——2024-2025年Agent"百花齐放"后，2026年的核心战场是可控性、可审计性和安全边界。DeepSlide 也从侧面印证：AI生成内容的价值评估标准，正从"输出了什么"（artifact）延伸到"如何交付和使用"（delivery）。
English
Two clear trends emerge. First, the renaissance of self-hosted infrastructure: Coolify (open-source PaaS), Immich (media management), Jellyfin, n8n (workflow automation), and Supabase all trended simultaneously, signaling a shift from public SaaS dependency to controllable on-premise deployment. Drivers include privacy compliance, long-term cost control, and vendor-lockin avoidance. Coolify alone supports 280+ one-click services, proving self-hosted UX now rivals managed platforms.

Second, AI Agents are evolving from functionality to governance: SDOF introduces state-constrained dispatch to eliminate the "alignment tax" in multi-agent orchestration, while SkillSmith compiles agent skills into boundary-guided runtime interfaces to prevent unauthorized invocation. Together they signal that after the 2024-2025 agent explosion, the 2026 battleground is controllability, auditability, and safety boundaries. DeepSlide reinforces this by shifting evaluation criteria from artifact quality to delivery experience.
