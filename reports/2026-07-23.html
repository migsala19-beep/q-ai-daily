English
Today's most notable direction is the dual evolution of "AI agent safety and scalability." First, the SysAdmin paper introduces a benchmark for measuring instrumental power-seeking in frontier AI systems, turning qualitative concerns like "resource acquisition, oversight evasion, and termination resistance" into quantifiable metrics. This is a crucial step from principles to measurement in AI safety research. Second, the BatchDAG paper addresses cross-entity enterprise analytics by proposing LLM-planned directed acyclic graphs (DAGs), solving context overflow, lost per-entity attribution, and linear latency. This aligns closely with real-world agentic AI needs: structured execution graphs offer a scalable alternative when single-document Q&A is insufficient for finance, compliance, or supply-chain scenarios. Meanwhile, GitHub trending projects such as Dioxus and Pyroscope reflect the continuing importance of full-stack and performance infrastructure as the foundation for AI application engineering.

- 规划代理工具注册中心时参考ToolDNS的DNS启发设计，理由：可扩展工具发现是代理生态的关键瓶颈，去中心化发现机制更具弹性。
- 关注Dioxus和Pyroscope等全栈/性能工具，理由：AI应用工程化不仅需要模型，还需要稳定、可观测的跨平台运行环境。
English
- Add power-seeking / termination-resistance stress tests to internal AI system evaluations. Rationale: SysAdmin provides a reproducible measurement framework to surface risky agentic behaviors early.
- Prioritize DAG execution graphs over linear chains when building enterprise data-analysis agents. Rationale: BatchDAG shows structured planning improves scalability and explainability for cross-entity queries.
- Introduce "evidence-chain confidence" and "selective abstention" in fact-checking products. Rationale: This prevents models from issuing forced verdicts when evidence is weak, reducing legal and reputational risk.
- Design agent tool registries using a DNS-inspired discovery mechanism like ToolDNS. Rationale: Scalable tool discovery is a critical bottleneck in agent ecosystems, and decentralized discovery is more resilient.
- Pay attention to full-stack and performance tools such as Dioxus and Pyroscope. Rationale: Productionizing AI applications requires stable, observable, cross-platform infrastructure, not just models.
