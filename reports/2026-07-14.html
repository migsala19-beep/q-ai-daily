🔬 深度研究报告

今日资讯呈现出两条清晰主线。其一，推理时控制（Inference-time Control）正在成为提升LLM系统可靠性的关键路径。CogniConsole提出将推理时控制形式化为独立抽象层，挑战了“可靠性仅取决于模型能力”的固有假设，意味着未来LLM应用架构可能向更模块化、可插拔的控制层演进。其二，Agent规划与长程任务评估同步深化。GATS通过图增强树搜索结合分层世界模型，降低规划过程中的LLM调用成本与随机性；Long-Horizon-Terminal-Bench则以密集奖励评分机制，测试Agent在长时间终端任务中的持续决策能力。两者共同推动AI Agent从完成短平快任务，向复杂、长程、可评估的自主工作流演进。此外，arXiv上关于多层感知机区间认证与Mean-Field理论的形式化研究，也显示AI安全与可解释性正吸引更多理论投入。
English
Two trends stand out. First, inference-time control is emerging as a critical path for LLM reliability. The CogniConsole paper externalizes this control as a formal abstraction, suggesting that reliability depends not just on model capability but on the computational layer governing task execution. This could shift LLM application design toward more modular, controllable architectures. Second, agent planning and long-horizon evaluation are advancing together. GATS combines graph-augmented tree search with layered world models to reduce cost and stochasticity, while Long-Horizon-Terminal-Bench uses dense reward-based grading to test agents on extended terminal tasks. Together they push agents from short tasks toward complex, sustained decision-making.
